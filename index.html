Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 27, 2026

Welcome to TinyTale Echoes. Your privacy and the safety of your children's memories are our highest priorities.

1. Data Collection & Storage

TinyTale Echoes is a local-first application. This means that all personal data (including child names, photos, quotes, and milestones) is stored exclusively on your device.

We do not have access to your data, we do not store it on our servers, and we do not sell it to third parties.

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

TinyTale Echoes is designed for use by parents and legal guardians. While the app stores information about children, this information is entered solely by the parents. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

3. Your Rights

Since all data remains on your device, you have full control over it. You can modify or delete any information, or delete the entire app at any time to remove all stored data.
